Very Chocolate Ice Cream

Description

This chocolate ice cream is a rich, custard-style ice cream flavored with semisweet chocolate and cocoa powder.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, to taste
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 lightly beaten egg yolks
  • Semisweet chocolate, chopped, to taste
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Bring the milk, sugar, cocoa, and salt to a simmer in a saucepan.
  2. Whisk 1/2 of the milk mixture into the yolks, return to the pan, and stir until thickened
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chopped chocolate.
  4. Refrigerate in a chilled bowl until cold.
  5. Add the remaining ingreadients, then freeze in an ice cream maker.
